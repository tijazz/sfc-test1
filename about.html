About SFC

Our history

The club was founded in 2015 by a group of 5 finance enthusiasts who were passionate about creating an association where other students can develop themselves and share opportunities. Over the years, the club has drawn over 60 active members across several disciplines in the University of Ibadan and has expanded to include 2 other divisions asides finance namely Human Resources/Consulting and Business/Enterpreneurship

MISSION

To train and produce dynamic and emerging student leaders in their respective industries

VISION

To be a leading student run finance club that develops her members to be employable and impactful actors globally
