About SFC

Our history

The club was founded in 2015 by a group of 5 finance enthusiasts who were passionate about creating an association where other students can develop themselves and share opportunities. Over the years, the club has drawn over 60 active members across several disciplines in the University of Ibadan and has expanded to include 2 other divisions asides finance namely Human Resources/Consulting and Business/Enterpreneurship

SFC

The Student Finance Club, University of Ibadan, is a highly organized, student-run institution, focused on preparing her members for success in professional roles at all levels of Finance, Human Resources & Consulting, and Business & Entrepreneurship by providing members with cutting edge skills and training relevant to their respective industries. The club serves as a central hub for connecting and developing students that are interested in developing themselves personally and professionally. SFC UI has a proven track record of excellence, having successfully produced members who have gone on to secure highly- competitive opportunities.
